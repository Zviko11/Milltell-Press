Who we are

Get to know about our company

Milltel Press (Pvt.) Limited is a legally established organisation, registered under the zimbabwe Companies Act (chapter 24:03). The company is young, dynamic and full of energy from strength to strength and to become a household name in the printing and stationary business.

Milltel Press(Pvt.) Limited also supply a wide range of ATM consumables ie. ATM reciept rolls, journal rolls, ATM cards, ATM ribbons and cleaning cards. The company also supply note counters, self seal currency bags and note detectors.
